In Memory of you
is a 2D interactive story
intended for a more
casual experience full of
emotion and reflection
that touches topics
such as depresion and grief.
Emerge on the story
about a young woman
named Aurora that
at a very young age
lost two important
people in her life in
a spawn of six months.
A game full of
emoticons and
messages that shows
that even at the
darkest times we can
overcome and find
ourselves again.
Aurora is a painter,
she is a determinated
dreamer with a soft
heart full of
criativity and hopes.
Iris is a mechanical
engeneer and Aurora's
best friend.
She is assertive
and is always there
protecting her friends
no matter the hardships.
A stubborn yet
caring person.
Rosa is Aurora's grandma
A generous soul that
teached and protected
her grandchild to be ready
for the hardships of life.
A role model in Aurora's eyes
