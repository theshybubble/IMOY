In Memory of you is
a 2D interactive story
intended for a more
casual experience.
It is a game full of
emotion and reflection
that touches topics such
as depresion and grief.
Emerge on the story
about a young woman
named Aurora that
at a very young age
lost two important
people in her life in
a spawn of six months.
A game full of
messages that shows
that even in the
darkest times we can
overcome and find
ourselves again.
Aurora is a painter.
She is a determinated
dreamer with a soft
heart full of
criativity and hopes.
Iris is a mechanical
engineer and Aurora's
best friend.
She is assertive
and is always there
protecting her friends
no matter the hardships.
A stubborn yet
caring person.
Rosa is Aurora's grandma.
A generous soul that
protected her grandchild
and teached her to
be ready for the
hardships of life.
A role model in
Aurora's eyes.
