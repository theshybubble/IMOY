In In Memory of You, we follow the main character’s journey
though grief and depression, after losing two very special
people in her life. By sharing Aurora’s story with the world,
our main go was to show that things do get better, even if
sometimes it feels like there’s no way out.

However important it is to regard dearly the ones we lost,
we must remember to appreciate the ones we still have
in our lives. Do not take the people you love for granted and
make memories with them whilst you still can.
Mental health (or emotional health) is just as important
as one’s physical health. We all get sad, stressed,
and frightened. Most of the time these feelings come to pass,
but sometimes they develop into more serious issues.

There is a stigma surrounding the topic and people
tend to get uncomfortable talking about it. That is why it is
important to spread awareness of its importance.
It is healthy to understand and talk about what you’re feeling.

The most common mental health problems are depression and anxiety,
but there are many other issues that can affect someone. However,
the absence of a diagnosable mental health problem does not mean
someone has a good mental help. For this reason, do not hesitate
on getting help if something does not seem right.

For more information about this,
visit Mental Health
If you are concerned that you are developing a
mental health problem, you should seek immediate help.
• Tell someone you trust.
• Talk to your family doctor.
• Get help from a professional.
